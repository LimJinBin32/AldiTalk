How it Works ▼

How It Works

This app integrates speech-to-text, text translation, and text-to-speech technologies to provide seamless language translation and communication. Here's a step-by-step overview:

1. Select Languages

Choose the source language and the target language for translation using the dropdown menus at the top of the app interface.

2. Input or Record Text

Type your text directly in the input box or press the microphone button to record your speech. The app supports automatic language detection for speech input.

3. Translate Text

Click the "Translate" button to process the input. The app uses Azure Cognitive Services and custom APIs to provide accurate translations in real-time.

4. View Translations

The translated text is displayed in the output area. If a common phrase is selected, the app populates both input and output areas accordingly.

5. Play Audio (Optional)

For supported languages, you can click the "Play Audio" button to listen to the translated text read aloud.

6. Clear Text

Use the "Clear Text" button to reset both input and output areas, allowing you to start a new translation task.

About Us ▼

About Us

At Azure Sage, we are dedicated to revolutionizing communication through cutting-edge speech-to-speech translation technology. Our project is designed to bridge language barriers, particularly for elderly residents at Apex Harmony Lodge (AHL), Singapore’s first purpose-built home for individuals with dementia.

Our Mission

Our goal is to enhance communication between caregivers and residents by developing a real-time, AI-powered translation tool. Many elderly individuals communicate in local dialects such as Hokkien and Cantonese, which caregivers may not understand. This language barrier can lead to misunderstandings and a reduced quality of care. Azure Sage is committed to bridging this gap, ensuring that every resident’s needs and preferences are clearly understood.

Why It Matters

Language should never be a barrier to care. By implementing speech-to-speech translation, we empower caregivers to communicate effectively with residents, fostering trust, comfort, and better well-being for individuals with dementia. Our solution aligns with AHL’s mission of person-centered care, enhancing the quality of life for residents and improving caregiving efficiency.

Our Commitment

Azure Sage is committed to continuous improvement. Our team actively researches data augmentation, model fine-tuning, and real-time performance optimization to ensure that our translation system remains accurate, efficient, and accessible for caregivers and residents alike.

Join us in breaking down language barriers—because everyone deserves to be heard and understood.
